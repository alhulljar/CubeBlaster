CubeBlaster
Version 1.3
---------------------------------------------------

Updates
---------------------------------------------------
Added Model for Gun and Bullets
Also Colors for Bullets and Gun

Movement
---------------------------------------------------
W: Forward
S: Back
A: Left
D: Right
Space: Jump
RMB: Shoot
Esc: Pause
Del: When pressed at Game Over screen resets High Score

Objective
---------------------------------------------------
Blast the colorful cubes and get the highest score possible!

Health: 30
Ammo: 20
